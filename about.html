About

Hi I'm Josh

I am a UX designer focused on human centred design. I am currently working on AIB's mobile banking app and website. I spend most of my time organising our internal design community of practice and early research and development.

Before working in AIB I worked in the Chicago Design Museum and The Lost Arts co working space. Both places gave me a great insight into Chicago's design scene and exposed me to other design processes and ways of working. While in Chicago I interview some designers from the Chicago Design Museum's network and made a small book on our talks.

My degree in Industrial Design has always made me interested in different disciplines and finding ways to collaberate. This curosity drives me to ask 'why' and push my designs to ask 'why not?'.
